# Marty Supreme Edition

A high-stakes, kinetic design system inspired by 1950s sporting editorial and mid-century brutalism. This system is designed to feel obsessive, self-assured, and precisely calculated.

## Visual Metaphor: The Match
The design treats every interface interaction as a "bout" or a "match." Precision and reflex are at the core of the aesthetic.

## Color Philosophy
- **Ink (#131313):** The dominant world. Used for deep backgrounds and visual weight.
- **Table (#1F3D2D):** Depth and structure. Used for secondary sections and "net" dividers.
- **Supreme (#FF4F00):** The ball. Used strictly for key moments, CTAs, and active states.
- **Paper (#F8F7F4):** The surface shift. Used for high-contrast sections that break the dark rhythm.
- **Sage (#7C9082):** The breath. Used for metadata, labels, and muted subtext.

## Typography
- **Display:** Arrogant, wide, and loud. Always all-caps.
- **Body:** Editorial and warm. Provides the soul to the brutalist layout.
- **Labels:** Tight, functional, and clinical. Used for scores, dates, and technical tags.

## Layout Rules
1. **The Net:** Use full-width scrolling marquees or heavy horizontal rules in Table Green to divide major narrative shifts.
2. **Contrast Sections:** "Paper" backgrounds should only appear for 1-2 specific sections to maximize impact.
3. **Punctuation:** The "Supreme" orange circle should be used as a singular, intentional focal point (the ball) in hero or footer areas.
